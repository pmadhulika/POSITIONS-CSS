Tirupathi

Tirupati is a city in Andhra Pradesh, famous for being a major Hindu pilgrimage site centered around the Tirumala Venkateswara Temple. It is called the "Spiritual Capital of Andhra Pradesh".

Goa

Goa is a state on India's western coast, known for its beaches and vibrant tourism industry. Goa's economy is supported by tourism, mining, and agriculture, and it features a tropical climate with a monsoon season from June to September.

Manali

Manali is a high-altitude Himalayan resort town in Himachal Pradesh, known for its stunning mountain views, lush forests, and the gushing Beas River. The town serves as a gateway for adventure sports

Kerala

Kerala is a tropical paradise in Southern India known for its beautiful beaches, tranquil backwaters, rolling hill stations, and rich culture. Its diverse landscape has earned it the nickname "God's Own Country".
Attractions: Alleppey, Kochi, Varkala, Munnar.

Rishikesh

Rishikesh is a city in the Himalayan foothills known as the "Yoga Capital of the World". On the banks of the sacred Ganges River, it is a hub for spiritual seekers and adventurers. The city is famous for its yoga and meditation centers,temples.

Agra

Agra, located on the banks of the Yamuna River, is a historic city and a major tourist destination. Agra was the capital of the Mughal Empire. Beyond its famous monuments, the city is a treasure trove of Mughal-era architecture, vibrant bazaars, and rich cultural heritage.
